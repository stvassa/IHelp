#IHelpUkrainianWomen
Julia Bernatska, owner of BIJB's Kyiv's office, has an urgent plea to assist the women and children who fled Ukraine.
BIJB is a woman-owned company based in Kyiv that mainly focuses on Microsoft Power BI analytics.

As a single mother, I am heartbroken to witness what is happening in my country. Millions of Ukrainian women with children are forced to flee the war, leaving behind their husbands and all they have to seek asylum in other countries. They are alone and have difficulty supporting and feeding themselves and their children.

Fear, pain, and despair are the emotions that now permeate every Ukrainian woman's heart. That is something I am fully aware of and fully comprehend how critical it is to give them a chance to get out of this challenging period and back on their feet.

As a result, our company, BIJB, has launched an initiative that can provide direct financial support to Ukrainian women who've been forced to flee military aggression.

We ask that you contribute to the support of Ukrainian refugees. Any contribution does have a significant impact. Together we can make a difference.
  
Where the funds are going:
Donations will be given to Ukrainian women who were forced to flee the war.

Priority is given to the most vulnerable - mothers with children under one year old.

The money will be transferred to their bank cards to provide financial support.
