Aid fund for Ukrainian women

The company BIJB advance the initiative to raise money for Ukrainian women with children, who were forced to escape from war. We are concerned about their financial possibility to feed themselves and their children.

Why do we help

Julia Bernatska owner of BIJB's woman-owned company based in Kyiv that mainly focuses on Microsoft Power BI analytics. Company has an urgent plea to assist the women and children who fled Ukraine. Millions of Ukrainian women with children are forced to flee the war, leaving behind all they have to seek asylum in other countries.

“ As a single mother, I am heartbroken to witness what is happening in my country.

Fear, pain, and despair are the emotions that now permeate every Ukrainian woman's heart. That is something I am fully aware of and fully comprehend how critical it is to give them a chance to get out of this challenging period and back on their feet. As a result, our company, BIJB, has launched an initiative that can provide direct financial support to Ukrainian women who've been forced to flee military aggression.

We ask that you contribute to the support of Ukrainian refugees. Any contribution does have a significant impact. Together we can make a difference.”

Julia Bernatska

How do we help

Financial addressed help

Female refugees are torn from usual life with the child in arms and backpack with documents. Vulnerable, bewildered and scared.They need some time to calm down and find a job.

Searching for a job can take from 2 to 6 months. During this time they need to feed and provide their children. Address financial help gives them time for pause, rehabilitation and job search.

Psychological Support

Confused mothers and their children need to tune in to new conditions, calm down and give support not only to their child but also to themselves. Our psychologist helps to adapt and replenish the resource.
