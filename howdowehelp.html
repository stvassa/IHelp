How do we help

Financial addressed help

Female refugees are torn from usual life with the child in arms and backpack with documents. Vulnerable, bewildered and scared.They need some time to calm down and find a job.

Searching for a job can take from 2 to 6 months. During this time they need to feed and provide their children. Address financial help gives them time for pause, rehabilitation and job search.

Psychological Support

Confused mothers and their children need to tune in to new conditions, calm down and give support not only to their child but also to themselves. Our psychologist helps to adapt and replenish the resource.
